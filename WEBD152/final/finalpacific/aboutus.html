Learn About Us

Our History

Founded in the 1970s, the Pacific Trails Resort started as a cozy family lodge. Over time, it grew into a sought-after destination, blending rustic charm with modern comforts, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in the stunning beauty of the nature of the Pacific coast.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide guests with an unforgettable and immersive experience amidst the natural wonders of the Pacific wilderness. Committed to environmental stewardship, the resort strives to preserve and protect the pristine surroundings while offering top-notch hospitality, ensuring a harmonious balance between modern comforts and the serenity of nature.

Our Team

Our team is a close-knit group of passionate individuals dedicated to providing exceptional service and hospitality. From the warm and welcoming front desk staff to the skilled chefs crafting delectable culinary delights, each member takes pride in ensuring guests' comfort and enjoyment.
