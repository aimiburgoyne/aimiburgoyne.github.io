Introduction

I was born in North Carolina into a military family with a Japanese mother. I moved a lot growing up, living in North Carolina, New York, Hawaii, Japan, and San Diego. My father retired when I was in middle school in San Diego, so I lived there the longest (6 years total). After graduating from high school in San Diego, I moved to Japan for college.

After graduating from college, I worked as an English teacher at a preschool for one year and then changed to my current job. I work in a recruiting firm as something called a "Senior Candidate Advisor". I have multiple consultants in my team who each work with their own clients and get the jobs, and then I work directly with people looking for jobs and introduce them to the roles my team works on.

For the past few years, I have been taking online courses at Mesa to continue learning new skills. I have always been interested in computer science and in college had my first experience with programming. It was a simple course that covered very basic HTML, CSS, and Javascript. However, that was around 6 years ago so I recently decided to change my major to web design and begin studying again. I took the beginning HTML and CSS course last semester and the content was mostly a (highly needed) refresher from what I learned in college. Through this class, I am hoping to expand my knowledge of HTML and CSS and learn new concepts.
